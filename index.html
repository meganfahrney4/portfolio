About Me

Megan Fahrney I am currently a master’s student at Northwestern University’s Medill School of Journalism with a specialization in Health, Science and Environment and a concentration in Business Reporting. I grew up in the Chicagoland area and received my undergraduate degree in Political Science and Global Affairs from the University of Notre Dame. I was a Fulbright Fellow in the West African nation of Benin in 2024-2025, where I wrote freelance articles on a variety of social, political and cultural subjects. I have held internships with the Council on Foreign Relations’ Editorial team and in the Global Public Affairs Bureau in the U.S. Department of State. As a reporter currently based in Chicago, I hope to cover topics such as energy transitions and technological developments.
